FAQ

How do I buy?

Click on the game and further click on add to cart, to further purchase the game you'll be sent to a secure website there you can add your credential information to finalize the purchase.

How do I register?

To register you need to create a username, put a valid email, your password and then confirm the password, furthermore you need to accept the terms of conditions in order to receive permission to register. After registration you will be sent to the login site where you can log in, there you can see the carts you have saved and further save them for future reference.
